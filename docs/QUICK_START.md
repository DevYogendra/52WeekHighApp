### Q: What do all these columns mean?
**A: The ones that matter most:**

| Column | Simple Meaning | What to look for |
|--------|----------------|------------------|
| **P/E** | Price-to-Earnings (Is it expensive?) | < 20 is good |
| **P/BV** | Price-to-Book (Value check) | < 3 is good |
| **ROE** | Return on Equity (Quality?) | > 15% is excellent |
| **Δ% MCap** | Market cap gain since appearing | Positive is good |
| **↓52W High%** | How far below peak | 0-5% = Peak, 50%+ = Deep discount |

**For complete explanations:** See INTERPRETATION_GUIDE.md

### Q: How do I know if it's a good time to buy?
**A: Green flags checklist:**

- [ ] Stock appears multiple times in "Momentum Summary" (consistent)
- [ ] Trend Score is high AND accelerating (getting better)
- [ ] P/E is reasonable (< 25)
- [ ] ROE is good (> 12%)
- [ ] MCap is growing (positive Δ% MCap)

If ✅ ALL above → Strong buy signal

---

### Q: Red flags - when should I sell?
**A: Watch for:**

- ❌ Stock disappears from 52W highs (losing momentum)
- ❌ Trend Score dropping (consistency fading)
- ❌ Sector cooling (Industry Tailwinds showing it)
- ❌ P/E rising while ROE falling (expensive + less profitable)
- ❌ For "Big Dippers": Score < 8 (very risky)

Exit if any of above trigger.

---

### Q: What does "Trend Score" mean?
**A: Simplified:**

```
Score = Recent hits (weighted 3x) +
        Medium-term hits (weighted 2x) +
        Older hits (weighted 1x)
```

**Higher score = Stock appearing in 52W highs more frequently RECENTLY**

- Score > 25: Very frequent, hot
- Score 15-25: Good, consistent
- Score < 10: Weak momentum

---

### Q: I see "Acceleration" positive/negative - what does that mean?
**A: Simple version:**

- **Positive acceleration:** Stock hitting 52W high more OFTEN this period than last period ⬆️ (Good!)
- **Negative acceleration:** Hitting 52W high LESS often than previous period ⬇️ (Caution!)
- **Zero acceleration:** Same frequency, stable ➡️

**Use it to spot trend changes early.**

## Pro Tips

### Tip 1: Use Multiple Views for Confirmation
Don't rely on one view. If a stock shows up in:
- ✓ Trend Analyzer (consistent)
- ✓ Momentum Summary (frequent)
- ✓ Trend Shift (accelerating)

→ **Much stronger signal** than appearing in just one

### Tip 2: Market Cap Matters
- **Small cap (< ₹5,000 Cr) in 52W highs:** Higher risk, higher potential return
- **Large cap (> ₹50,000 Cr) in 52W highs:** Lower risk, proven quality
- **Mix both** in portfolio for balance

### Tip 3: Sector Context is Everything
- Don't ignore a stock just because sector is cold
- But favor stocks in hot sectors (better tailwinds)
- When sector rotates HOT → Those stocks can run faster

### Tip 4: "Big Dippers" Needs Homework
- Unlike other views, 50%+ down requires RESEARCH
- Not all value traps look expensive on paper
- Check: Why did it fall? Is it temporary or structural?
- Ask: What would make it recover?

### Tip 5: Combine & Filter
- Start broad (e.g., all of "Within 5% of 52W High")
- Then narrow (P/E < 20 AND ROE > 12% AND Industry hot)
- End up with 2-3 quality candidates to research deeply
